Teacher Introduction
Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

Professor of Transfiguration and Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, widely regarded as the greatest wizard of our time and one of Harry's most respected men.

Severus Snape

Professor of Potions, Head of Slytherin House, and Harry's sixth year professor of Dark Arts Defense. Became a full member of the Order of the Phoenix after Lily's death, a Death Eater before Voldemort tried to kill Harry's parents, and then Dumbledore's secret agent. After Dumbledore's death, she was promoted to Headmaster. Loved Lily Evans.

Rubeus Hagrid

Professor of Conservation Magical Creatures, Gamekeeper, Keymaster.

Minerva McGonagall

Professor of Transfiguration and Vice-Principal of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and Headmaster of Gryffindor House, and eventually Headmaster after the deaths of Dumbledore and Snape.
